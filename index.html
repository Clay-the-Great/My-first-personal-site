Hello.

Chenmeng Wang (wangchenmeng09@gmail.com) received the Ph.D. degree in information and telecommunication engineering from the Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT), Chongqing, China, in 2018. From 2019 to 2021, he was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Alberta, Edmonton, AB, Canada. From 2015 to 2017, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at Carleton University, Ottawa, ON, Canada. His research interests include small-cell HetNets, multi-access edge computing, massive MIMO systems and resource allocation in mobile networks.
